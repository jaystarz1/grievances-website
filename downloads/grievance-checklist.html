Pre-Filing Checklist

  • Identified the specific decision, act, or omission you're grieving
  • Confirmed you're within the 90-day time limit
  • Attempted informal resolution (if appropriate)
  • Gathered all relevant documentation
  • Identified applicable policies, regulations, or orders
  • Considered seeking legal advice or union representation

Filing Stage Checklist

  • Completed grievance form (paper or digital)
  • Written clear, specific contentions
  • Attached all supporting documentation
  • Identified the Initial Authority
  • Kept copies of everything for your records
  • Submitted within timeline requirements

During the Process

  • Responded to all requests for information promptly
  • Tracked all correspondence and dates
  • Maintained professional communication
  • Reviewed disclosure packages thoroughly
  • Submitted representations within deadlines
  • Kept detailed notes of all interactions

Final Authority Stage

  • Reviewed Initial Authority decision carefully
  • Identified grounds for escalation (if needed)
  • Submitted to Final Authority within 90 days
  • Provided any new evidence or arguments
  • Considered MGERC review eligibility
